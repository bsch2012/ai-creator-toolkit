# AI Creator Toolkit

Open-source pipelines and workflows for AI-powered video content creation.

## Available Workflows
- 🎬 BSCH.VAULT System (CRT TV character automation)
- 🎨 Kling AI + Hedra + InVideo integration guides
- ✍️ Caption Forge templates (Instagram/TikTok optimized)
- 📝 Tutorial bounty documentation (Hedra, OpenArt, InVideo)

## Tools Covered

### Video Generation & Production
- **InVideo AI** - Text-to-video instant creator (script → publish-ready in 3-5 min)
  - Sora 2 integration (60s photorealistic)
  - VEO 3.1 (character consistency)
  - AI Twins (voice cloning 50+ languages)
  - Magic Box editing (natural language commands)
  
- **Kling AI** - AI video generation (raw clips & scenes)
- **Hedra** - Character animation (lip-sync & facial animation)

### Image & Asset Generation
- **OpenArt** - AI image generation (LoRA, custom models)
- **Wavespeed** - LoRA workflow & training

### Editing & Post-Production
- **CapCut** - Editing automation & timeline optimization

## Use Cases
- Short-form video automation (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- AI news content (@bsch.vault style)
- Tutorial & educational content (faceless YouTube)
- Product demos & marketing ads
- Bounty hunting workflows (Hedra, InVideo challenges)

## Tool Comparison

| Tool | Best For | Output Time | Skill Level |
|------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| InVideo AI | Full video production (instant) | 3-5 min | Beginner |
| Kling AI | Custom AI video clips | 5-15 min | Intermediate |
| Hedra | Character animation & lip-sync | 2-5 min | Beginner |
| CapCut | Final editing & polish | 30+ min | Intermediate |
