# BSCH.VAULT Workflow Overview

AI-powered short-form video content system featuring CRT TV-headed characters.

## Characters
- **CRT Signal** - AI news & tech updates
- **CRT Static** - Creator tools & tutorials  
- **CRT Raw** - Behind-the-scenes & experiments

## Production Pipeline

### 1. Concept & Script
- Caption Forge templates
- Hook-first structure (visual > narration)
- 15-60 second format

### 2. Asset Generation
- **Kling AI** - Video clips & scenes
- **Hedra** - Character animation & lip-sync
- **OpenArt** - Background & visual elements
- **Wavespeed** - LoRA training (RETROLARMA style)

### 3. Post-Production
- **CapCut** - Editing & timeline
- Audio sync & effects
- Color grading (CRT aesthetic)

### 4. Distribution
- Instagram (@bsch.vault)
- TikTok
- YouTube Shorts

## Tools Used
- Kling AI (video generation)
- Hedra (character animation)
- OpenArt (image generation)
- CapCut (editing)
- FlowithAI (workflow automation)

## Style Guide
- 9:16 vertical format
- CRT TV aesthetic
- Bold, edgy visuals
- Plain-text captions (no smart quotes)

Active development - targeting bounty programs (Hedra tutorial bounty)
